Strategy Lab

Test a trading idea in everyday language. We match it to your journal (or show clear demo examples) so you can see what worked — not a mysterious black-box score.

What this does

Market mode: runs your idea on real daily candles (Yahoo / ECB Frankfurter free FX, or Twelve Data if you add an API key). Journal mode: scores trades you already logged. Results show step-by-step stories — not a black-box score.

Market data notes
  • Free (default): Yahoo daily OHLC when rate limits allow; forex also via ECB Frankfurter (no API key).
  • Cached: successful downloads are stored on disk for ~24h so Lab stays fast and resilient.
  • Optional API: set TWELVEDATA_API_KEY for gold/indices/crypto coverage and higher limits.
  • Intraday (15M): needs a paid market feed; daily is the honest free baseline.

Always compare market backtests to your tagged Playbook journal — your live execution is the real edge.
