Roadmap: real market backtesting

To go from “journal match” to automatic chart backtesting, the strongest path is:

  1. Import OHLC history (or connect a data feed) for the symbols you trade.
  2. Turn your Playbook rules into checks (e.g. “price swept below support then closed back above”).
  3. Replay bar-by-bar and mark every valid entry, stop, and target on a chart.
  4. Report expectancy: win rate, average R, max drawdown, best session — then compare to your live journal.

Until that ships: use Lab + Playbook + tagged trades — that already proves whether you follow the setup well.
