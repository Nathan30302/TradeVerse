Beginners journal many setups to learn; experienced traders focus on 2–3 high grades (A++ / A+ / A− / B+) — the ~20% of patterns that often drive ~80% of profits. They won’t fire every day — wait for them. Add chart examples, then select this setup every time you take the trade. Test the wording in Strategy Lab.
