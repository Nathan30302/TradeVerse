What it is

A trading journal is memory you can trust

Brokers show fills. Charts show price. A journal shows you — why you entered, how you felt, whether you followed your rules, and what to change next time.

Without one, the same mistakes repeat. With TradeVerse, every session writes a story you can reopen in five years — filter by symbol, emotion, setup, or a quiet Wednesday — and improve from real evidence, not memory.

  • See which setups actually make money
  • Catch revenge trading and FOMO in the data
