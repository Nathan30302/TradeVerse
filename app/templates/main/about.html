About {{ app_name }}

{{ app_name }} is built for traders who want a clear record of what they did in the market — and a repeatable way to improve. You get a structured journal, dashboards that summarize performance, and tools to connect planning with execution.

What you can do here

  • Log trades — entries, exits, risk, notes, and screenshots so each setup is documented.
  • Use the trade planner — plan before you execute, then review after the trade so lessons stick.
  • Track performance — dashboards and analytics help you see win rate, drawdown-style context, and patterns over time.
  • Import and stay organized — bring history in from statements or integrations where available, so the journal matches your real account activity.

Who it is for

Whether you trade forex, indices, crypto, or other markets, the same habits matter: preparation, execution, and honest review. {{ app_name }} focuses on those habits instead of noise — so you can see what actually moves your results week to week.

Plans and support

Start with core journaling and upgrade when you want advanced analytics, exports, or other Pro capabilities. If you have product feedback or need help, use the contact page — we read real messages from traders.
